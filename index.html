Frequently asked questions

What age range is this for?

ParentFlare covers ages 2–11, split into three groups: Toddlers (2–4), Little Kids (5–7), and Big Kids (8–11). Activities and personalized results are tailored to your child's age group.

Do I need an account to use it?

No. The free version works instantly — no sign-up, no account, no email required. Premium features require an email for billing through Stripe.

What's the difference between free and Premium?

Free gives you the full activity library plus custom activities and coaching scripts (single results). Premium adds follow-up conversations so you can adjust results in real time, plus "Plan my time" — a personalized trip and day planner that creates custom activity plans based on your location, time, and energy level.

How is this different from other parenting apps?

Most activity apps are designed for calm, planned moments — browsing Pinterest-style lists when you have time to prepare. ParentFlare is built for right now. You open it when you have unplanned time and need something immediately. No browsing, no saving for later, no prep lists. Just what to do and what to say, in seconds.

Is my data safe?

We don't sell your data. Email addresses are used only for Stripe billing. Your conversations and generated content are not stored or used for training. See our full Privacy Policy below.

If your child has specific medical, behavioral, or developmental needs, please consult a qualified professional.

Free and Premium Plans

ParentFlare offers a free tier that includes access to 85+ curated activities, AI-generated custom activities, and AI-generated coaching scripts. No account or payment is required to use the free tier.

ParentFlare Premium is an optional paid subscription at $10.00 USD per month. Premium subscriptions begin with a 7-day free trial. After the trial, you are billed monthly on the date your trial ends. When you cancel after the trial, your Premium access continues until the end of your current billing period. There are no cancellation fees, no long-term commitments, and no questions asked.

Because ParentFlare Premium provides immediate access to AI-generated content upon subscription, refunds are generally not provided for partial billing periods. Privacy Policy

ParentFlare collects minimal data to provide and improve the service.

Free users: We do not require an account or collect personal information to use the free features.
