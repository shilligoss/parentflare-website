What ParentFlare Is

ParentFlare is a web-based application that provides parents with a curated activity library, a custom activity generator, coaching scripts, and a personalized trip and day planner (Plan my time). The service is available at parentflare.vercel.app and described at parentflare.com.

What ParentFlare Is Not

ParentFlare is not a medical, therapeutic, psychological, or clinical service. The activities and parenting scripts provided are general suggestions based on common child development principles. They are not a substitute for professional advice from a pediatrician, therapist, psychologist, or other licensed healthcare provider. If your child has specific medical, behavioral, or developmental needs, please consult a qualified professional.

Free and Premium Plans

ParentFlare offers a free tier that includes access to 85+ curated activities, custom activities, and coaching scripts. No account or payment is required to use the free tier. Creating an optional account enables personalized suggestions based on your family profile.

ParentFlare Premium is an optional paid subscription at $5.00 USD per month. Premium adds follow-up conversations (up to 5 turns per session) for the activity generator and coaching script features, plus "Plan my time" — a personalized trip and day planner that creates custom activity plans. Premium subscriptions are processed securely through Stripe.

Billing and Cancellation

Premium subscriptions begin with a 7-day free trial. After the trial, you are billed monthly on the date your trial ends. You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account profile page or Stripe Customer Portal. If you cancel during the trial, you will not be charged. When you cancel after the trial, your Premium access continues until the end of your current billing period. There are no cancellation fees, no long-term commitments, and no questions asked.

Because ParentFlare Premium provides immediate access to AI-generated content upon subscription, refunds are generally not provided for partial billing periods. If you experience a technical issue that prevents you from using Premium features, please contact us at hello@parentflare.com and we will make it right.

AI-Generated Content

The "Make me one," "Plan my time," and "Find the words" features use AI (powered by Anthropic's Claude) to generate custom activities, plans, and parenting scripts. AI-generated content is provided as personalized suggestions only. You know your child best — always use your own judgment before applying any suggestion. ParentFlare is not responsible for outcomes resulting from the use of AI-generated content.
